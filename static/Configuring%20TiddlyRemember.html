Configuring TiddlyRemember

23rd May 2020 at 1:03pm
TiddlyRemember

TiddlyRemember configuration is found in two places, in Anki and in your wiki(s).

  • Add-on configuration within Anki is used to list the wikis you want to sync to your Anki collection. You have to set up the add-on configuration before you can sync anything.
  • Mapping tiddlers within your wiki(s) are used to determine what decks and tags your notes will have in Anki. Mapping tiddlers are entirely optional – if you don't need different decks and tags in Anki, just ignore them.
