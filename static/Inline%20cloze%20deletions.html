Inline cloze deletions

23rd May 2020 at 1:20pm
Cloze notes

Cloze deletions can be rendered inline, as part of a paragraph in your wikitext, rather than as a separate paragraph. This has no effect on the note generated in Anki, and it looks like this in your wiki: {cloze: To render a cloze deletion inline, simply add the {inline} third argument to the remembercz macro. } To render a cloze deletion inline, simply add the {inline} third argument to the remembercz macro.
