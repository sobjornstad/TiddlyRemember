Installing the Anki plugin

23rd May 2020 at 11:14am
  1. Browse to the Anki add-on's homepage.
  2. Scroll down to find the add-on's code in the blue box, and copy this to your clipboard.
  3. In Anki, choose Tools > Add-ons > Get Add-ons.
  4. Paste the code into the box and click OK.
  5. Quit and restart Anki.
